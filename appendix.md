# Appendix: AI Drafts and Revisions

This shows some examples of the AI-generated content and how I edited it.

## Website Structure

I asked ChatGPT: "What pages should I include in a portfolio website for an MBA student?"

The AI suggested:
- Home/About page
- Resume page  
- Projects page
- Skills page
- Contact page

I kept this structure because it made sense and covered everything I needed to show.

## Home Page Example

**AI's first draft:**
"Hello! I'm an MBA student with a passion for data analytics. I enjoy working with data and solving business problems."

**Problem:** Way too generic and didn't sound like me at all.

**My revision:**
"I'm currently pursuing my MBA at University Canada West, focusing on business analytics and data-driven decision making. What excites me most is taking complex business problems and finding insights through data analysis."

I made it more specific and conversational.

## Work Experience Description

**AI's draft:**
"Worked on various data analysis projects. Helped with forecasting and financial analysis."

**Problem:** Too vague, didn't explain what I actually did.

**My version:**
"Worked with senior analysts on various data projects, mainly focused on sales forecasting and financial analysis. My role involved analyzing historical data, creating visualizations, and helping present findings to stakeholders."

Added actual details about the work I did.

## Projects Description

**AI's draft:**
"This was a project about BYD entering the Canadian market. I analyzed the electric vehicle market and looked at competition."

**Problem:** Way too short and didn't show what I actually learned or did.

**My version:**
"For my MBA program, I worked on analyzing how BYD could enter the Canadian electric vehicle market. This was a really interesting project because it involved looking at everything from competitive dynamics to regulatory challenges."

Made it more detailed and showed what made the project interesting.

## Skills Page

**AI's draft:**
Just listed: "Python, Excel, Tableau, Business Analytics, Problem Solving, Communication"

**Problem:** Just a list with no context.

**My version:**
Organized into categories (Technical Skills, Analytical Abilities, Business Skills) and added brief descriptions of what I use each tool for.

This gave more context about my actual capabilities.

## What I Changed Overall

The AI gave me a good starting structure, but I had to:
- Rewrite almost everything to sound like me
- Add all the specific details about my actual work and projects
- Make the tone more conversational and less formal
- Remove generic phrases that could apply to anyone
- Add personal touches about what I find interesting

The technical stuff (CSS, Markdown formatting) the AI handled well. The content needed a lot of work to make it authentic.
